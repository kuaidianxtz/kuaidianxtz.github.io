Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2026

Quick Clicker ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Data Storage

The App is a purely local utility tool. We do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information. Operation records, settings parameters, and other data you save in the App are stored solely on your local device.

1.2 Permissions Usage

To provide core functionality, we require the following system permissions:

  • Accessibility Service Permission: Used to implement auto-tap, swipe, and other gesture operations. This is the core functionality of the App.
  • Overlay Permission (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Used to display the control floating window, allowing you to start/stop auto-tapping at any time.
  • Storage Permission (Optional): Only required when you actively export operation records, used for reading/writing local files.

All permissions are manually authorized by you in system settings. We do not force or secretly obtain them.

1.3 Log Information

The App does not send log information to any servers. Temporary logs generated during App operation are only used for local debugging and are not persistently stored or uploaded.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your information for any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality locally on your device.

2.2 All data processing occurs entirely on your device. We have no access to your operation records, settings, or other data.

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 All data (including operation records, configuration parameters, etc.) is stored in your device's local storage.

3.2 We do not transmit your data to any remote servers or cloud services.

3.3 When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted along with the App.

4. How We Share, Transfer, or Publicly Disclose Your Information

4.1 Sharing: We do not share your personal information with any company, organization, or individual.

4.2 Transfer: We do not transfer your personal information to any company, organization, or individual.

4.3 Public Disclosure: We do not publicly disclose your personal information. If disclosure is absolutely necessary, we will inform you of the purpose and type of disclosure and proceed only after obtaining your explicit consent.

5. How We Protect Your Information

5.1 Since data is stored entirely locally, your information security is primarily protected by your device's system security measures.

5.2 We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device secure to prevent unauthorized access by others
  • Regularly backup important data
  • Do not lend your device to untrusted individuals

5.3 Despite our implementation of reasonable security measures, please understand that due to technological limitations and various potentially malicious means, even with utmost efforts to strengthen security measures, we cannot guarantee 100% information security.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Deletion Rights: You can view, modify, or delete your saved operation records in the App at any time.

6.2 Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw permission authorization for the App at any time in system settings. After withdrawal, the App may not function properly with related features, but this does not affect information processing that has already occurred based on your prior authorization.

6.3 Right to Deletion: You can delete your usage by uninstalling the App. After uninstallation, all local data will be cleared.

9.1 We may revise this Policy from time to time. When material changes are made to this Policy, we will notify you through in-App notifications or other appropriate means.

9.2 Material changes include but are not limited to:

10. International Data Transfers

10.1 Since all data is processed and stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers involved.

10.2 If you are located outside the jurisdiction where the developer is located, please note that your data remains on your device and is not transferred to any external servers.

11.1 GDPR Compliance: For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not process personal data, many GDPR requirements are not applicable, but we respect your rights to access, rectify, and delete data stored locally.
